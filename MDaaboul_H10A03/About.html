Our Story

Origins

Olie Paaboul was the reason why OPSONS exists in the first place. He was an inventor that worked with massive corporations that had big teams. He was able to figure out the things they couldn't.

Family

Olie Paaboul gave birth to 14 sons and daughters, 2 of the sons we're very intrested in cars. They're name are Borge and Gassam. Their father taught them everything on how to fix cars, by making a book full of knowledge.

Beginning of it all

The 2 brothers moved to Canada with the idea to settle down and get a normal job, this all changed when they saw the opportunity to combine forces and create a company, based off the name of their father Olie Paaboul. They built it knowing that they would gain the trust of theirs customers and never spent a dime on advertising, growing using purely word of mouth. This way we know that we are keeping our promise.
